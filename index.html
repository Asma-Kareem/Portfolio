About Me

Science Meets Purpose

A chemistry graduate with a strong passion for environmental sustainability and laboratory research. Seeking to apply scientific knowledge in laboratory analysis, education, and community engagement through impactful, eco-conscious projects that support public health and sustainable development.

My journey from the laboratory benches of the University of Duhok to community outreach across the Kurdistan region reflects a core belief: that science is most powerful when it serves people. I combine rigorous analytical thinking with a genuine drive to connect research findings to real-world impact.

Whether coordinating innovation hubs with UNICEF, testing household water quality for 222 families, or leading tree-planting campaigns across Duhok — I bring both technical precision and human warmth to every initiative.
